Legend:
+ = Corner characters (ACS_ULCORNER, ACS_URCORNER, ACS_LLCORNER, ACS_LRCORNER)
- = Horizontal line (ACS_HLINE)
| = Vertical line (ACS_VLINE)
# = Block character (ACS_BLOCK)

Text Attributes:
- Normal text: Plain rendering
- Bold text: Rendered with A_BOLD attribute
- Reverse text: Rendered with A_REVERSE attribute (inverted colors)
- Standout text: Rendered with A_STANDOUT attribute (highlighted)
